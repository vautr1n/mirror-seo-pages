# Mirror for Superhuman Users

Superhuman is the fastest email client ever built. It's engineered for speed: AI-powered auto-drafts, lightning-fast navigation, one-keystroke search. If you use Superhuman, you've probably saved four hours a week on email alone.

But speed alone isn't the full picture.

## The Superhuman + Mirror Story

Superhuman handles the *mechanics* of email. It gets you through your inbox fast. Mirror handles the *intelligence* of email — the context that separates a good response from a great one.

Imagine this: An email lands from Sarah Chen. Superhuman gets you there in two seconds. Then what?

With just Superhuman, you know:
- Her name, email address, and maybe her past subject lines
- What she's writing about *right now*

With Mirror, you know:
- Every project you've worked on together
- The promises you made, and what's still unresolved
- The evolution of your working relationship
- Context from your shared calendar — what meeting is happening when
- What her priorities are, drawn from your entire communication history with her

That's the difference between **fast** and **intelligent**.

Superhuman makes email *efficient*. Mirror makes email *effective*.

## Why Superhuman + Mirror Works

You've chosen Superhuman because you respect your time. You don't want to waste it on email. That's your operating principle: speed matters.

But here's the insight: **the real waste in email isn't the time you spend reading. It's the mistakes you make from lack of context.**

The email you send without remembering what you promised in last week's meeting. The follow-up you forgot to send because the commitment was buried in a thread. The opportunity you missed because you didn't have the full picture of someone's priorities. The misunderstanding that escalates because you didn't know the relationship had changed.

Those aren't speed problems. Superhuman can't solve them, even though it's perfect at its job.

Mirror sees your entire email history with someone — not just the subject lines, but the narrative. It knows:
- What's been decided and what's still pending
- Who depends on what
- The trajectory of each relationship and project
- What each person cares about (drawn from their emails, not from guessing)

When you open an email in Superhuman, Mirror is quietly working in the background: *Here's everything you need to know about this person and this project, updated in real time.*

## What Mirror Adds to Your Superhuman Workflow

**Pre-meeting briefings.** Before you hop on a call with someone, Mirror surfaces the context: recent commitments, open questions, the current state of projects you share. You come to the meeting prepared, not scrambling to remember.

**Intelligent follow-ups.** Mirror extracts the action items buried in email threads — the things that were agreed to, the questions that were asked. It reminds you when follow-ups are due, based on what was promised.

**Relationship intelligence.** You see not just an individual email, but the pattern: how your relationship with this person is evolving, what they care about, what's in progress. Over time, you know someone better because you have a coherent view of your entire history together.

**Context resurrection.** Months later, someone references something from an old thread. Instead of digging through subject lines (even fast in Superhuman), Mirror knows the full context instantly: "This refers to the project you both worked on in Q3, which was about X."

**Decision clarity.** Email is full of decisions buried in prose. Mirror extracts them: "You committed to shipping the API by end of month. Here's who needs it and when."

None of this slows you down. Mirror doesn't make you *slower* at email — it makes you *smarter* about it.

## Superhuman's Speed + Mirror's Context = The Complete Email Experience

Superhuman users understand that tools are about leverage. You chose Superhuman because it multiplies your email efficiency. Mirror multiplies your email *effectiveness*.

Together, they're more than the sum of their parts:

- **Superhuman** moves you through your inbox in record time
- **Mirror** makes sure that when you respond, you're operating from full context

You save time with Superhuman. You make better decisions with Mirror.

## How Mirror Connects to Your Superhuman Workflow

Mirror ingests your email data through a secure, encrypted connection (and connects to your calendar, notes, and meeting recorders for full context). It doesn't replace Superhuman — it sits alongside it, building a living knowledge graph of your relationships and commitments.

When you open an email in Superhuman, Mirror is there:
- As a side panel that surfaces relevant context
- As pre-meeting briefings that show up in your calendar
- As proactive reminders about commitments and follow-ups
- As a searchable knowledge base of your professional relationships

You stay in Superhuman's elegant, fast interface. Mirror works in the background, making sure you're never operating from partial information.

## For Superhuman Power Users: The Competitive Advantage

If you're already using Superhuman, you've already optimized for speed. Adding Mirror is how you optimize for impact.

Speed gives you *options*. Context gives you *judgment*. Combined, they're unstoppable.

Your competitors might have Superhuman. But they don't have your institutional memory, your relationship intelligence, your knowledge graph of commitments and decisions. Mirror is the moat that turns email from a necessary task into a competitive advantage.

## The Mirror Advantage: Portable, Independent, Yours

Mirror isn't locked into Superhuman, Gmail, Slack, or any single platform. It's a *personal context layer* that lives on your side of the wall. Your context is yours. Your relationships are yours. Your data isn't held hostage by any platform.

As you grow and your tools change, Mirror moves with you. It's the persistent layer underneath, independent of what email client you use or what AI you're working with.
