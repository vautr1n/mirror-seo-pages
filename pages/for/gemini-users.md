# Mirror for Gemini Users

Gemini has the deepest integration with Google Workspace of any AI.

If you live in Gmail, Google Docs, Google Calendar, and Google Meet — which millions of knowledge workers do — Gemini is frictionless. It's there, native, woven into the tools you already use. No context switching. No logging into a different app. Gemini works where you work.

That integration is powerful. It's also a trap.

Because Gemini's context lives inside Google. If your AI intelligence is locked inside a platform, what happens when that platform changes its mind?

We know what happens. OpenClaw users found out the hard way.

## The Platform Risk Behind Deep Integration

In February 2026, Google banned OpenClaw users whose usage violated its terms of service, cutting off accounts without warning. One policy change, and hundreds of users lost access. Their AI integrations stopped working. Their workflows broke. The context they'd built on Gemini was suddenly inaccessible.

This wasn't a bug. It was a policy enforcement. Google has every right to make that decision. But the users — the people who built their workflows on top of Gemini — had no choice and no recourse.

That's the cost of putting your AI intelligence inside someone else's platform.

## Why Context Portability Matters

Context is your competitive advantage. It's your knowledge graph. It's your understanding of your relationships, your projects, your commitments. Over time, you build intelligence that's genuinely valuable.

If that intelligence lives inside Gemini, you're betting that:
- Google will always want to support your use case
- Google's policies won't change
- Google won't decide to monetize or restrict your access
- Your context will always be retrievable if you ever leave

That's a lot of betting on a company's future decisions.

## The Mirror Advantage: Your Context Is Yours

Mirror is built on a different principle: your context lives on your side of the wall.

Mirror connects to your Google Workspace if you want. It reads your Gmail, your Calendar, your Meet recordings. But the knowledge graph that Mirror builds — your relationships, your commitments, your projects, your intelligence — that lives with Mirror, not with Google.

If you decide to use a different AI. If you switch to a different email provider. If you change tools. Your context moves with you.

It's portable. It's independent. It's yours.

## What Mirror Does That Gemini Can't

Gemini is powerful because it's integrated. You're in Gmail, Gemini surfaces relevant information from your inbox. You're writing a Doc, Gemini helps you write. You're on a Meet call, Gemini takes notes.

But Gemini's intelligence is trapped inside Google's tools. It can't go beyond Google Workspace.

Mirror goes everywhere:
- Email (Gmail, but also Superhuman, Outlook, others)
- Communication (Slack, Discord, Teams, Telegram)
- Calendars and meetings (Google Calendar, Outlook, others)
- Notes and documents (Google Docs, Notion, Obsidian, others)
- Meeting recordings (any provider)

Mirror builds a knowledge graph of your entire professional life, not just the parts that live in Google.

Then Mirror prepares your personal context so you can bring it to *any* AI:
- Gemini (if you want to keep using it)
- Claude (if you switch)
- ChatGPT (if you prefer it)
- Whatever AI you use next

You're not locked in. Your context is portable.
