# Mirror for Copilot Users: Personal Context Beyond Microsoft

## Your AI assistant is everywhere. Except where you are.

Copilot is embedded in everything Microsoft ships. Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams. It summarizes your emails, drafts your docs, catches you up on meetings, even pulls trends from your spreadsheets. If your entire life lived inside Microsoft 365, Copilot would be enough.

But it doesn't.

## What Copilot sees. What it misses.

Copilot has access to your Microsoft data. It knows your inbox, your documents, your meeting notes. It uses them to build context for the moment—draft that email faster, explain that Excel formula, recap that Teams call.

What Copilot doesn't see is everything else.

Your Slack threads about who owns what. Your personal Gmail conversations with stakeholders outside your company. Your Notion workspace where you actually track commitments. Your Claude conversations where you think through problems. Your calendar across multiple systems. Your past decisions and the reasons behind them.

Copilot gives you summaries of your Outlook inbox. It doesn't tell you the full relationship history with someone. It recaps a Teams meeting. It doesn't tell you what you promised them last month. It summarizes a document. It doesn't tell you how it connects to three other initiatives you're running.

That gap is where you lose time. That gap is where you miscommunicate. That gap is where you miss the context that matters.

## How Mirror + Copilot work together.

Mirror builds what Copilot can't: a context graph of your relationships, commitments, and decisions across every tool you use.

**Cross-tool context.** Copilot works inside Microsoft. Mirror unifies Microsoft with Slack, Gmail, Notion, Claude, Salesforce, HubSpot, Linear, your calendar, your CRM. When you ask Mirror about someone, it doesn't just show you emails from Outlook—it shows you emails, Slack threads, project updates, past conversations, and what you've committed to across every tool where those conversations happened.

**Relationship intelligence.** Copilot can summarize an email thread. Mirror understands the relationship inside it. How long have you known this person? What have you committed to with them? What matters to them based on past conversations? What was the context of the last exchange? When you open a meeting invite or email, Mirror delivers that relational intelligence to Copilot so it can do its job better.

**Works with every AI.** Copilot only works in Microsoft apps. Mirror feeds your context graph to Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini—any AI you use. You build your context once. Every AI you work with gets smarter.

**Enterprise AND personal.** Copilot is your company's AI assistant. Your company controls it, configures it, secures it. Mirror is YOUR AI—personal, portable, yours to shape. Copilot is a product. Mirror is your Artificial Personal Intelligence.

## The bottom line.

Copilot is your Microsoft assistant. It's smart, fast, and built into the tools you use every day. Mirror is your personal context layer. It works alongside Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, and every other AI you use. It knows you—your relationships, your commitments, your decisions—across every tool in your workflow.

Copilot + Mirror means you show up to every conversation, every meeting, every email with the full picture. You don't miss context. You don't double-commit. You don't ask for information you already have.
