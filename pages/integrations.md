# Mirror works with the tools you already use.

Mirror doesn't ask you to change your workflow. It builds a personal memory layer underneath it — then feeds that memory back to you at the moments it matters.

Connect your email and calendar in 90 seconds. Mirror does the rest.

---

## How it works

Mirror connects to the apps you already rely on. It reads the signals — emails, calendar events, meeting transcripts, notes — and builds a living knowledge graph of **people**, **topics**, **commitments**, and **relationships**.

Then it gives that context back to you: before meetings, every morning, across every tool. No new interface to learn. No prompts to write.
